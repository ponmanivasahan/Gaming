How To Play

Game Rules

  • 2 player game on one keyboard or can play vs Co-op
  • Win 2 out of 3 rounds to claim the match
  • Reduce the opponent's health to zero to win a round instantly
  • Or have more health when the 30-seconds timer runs out
  • Earn coins each round you win and you can spend them in shop to buy power-ups

Player 1 Controls

  • A - Move Left
  • D -Move Right
  • Z -Jump
  • Shift- Attack
  • SpaceBar-Block(reduce damage to 5)
  • E-Ultimate Attack(when charge bar is full)

Player 2 Controls

  • Left Arrow-Move Left
  • Right Arrow-Move Right
  • Up Arrow-Jump
  • Down Arrow-Attack
  • CTRL-Block(reduce damage to 5)
  • Enter-Ultimate Attack(when charge bar is full)

Power-ups HOTKEYS

  • Items are offered before each round via pop-up or active mid-round:
  • P1 Keys:1= Shield  |  2 = Health Boost  |  3 = Damage Boost
  • P1 Keys:4= Speed Boost  |  5 = Time Freeze  |  6 = Invinciblity
  • P2 Keys:7= Shield  |  8 = Health Boost  |  9 = Damage Boost
  • P2 Keys:0 = Speed Boost  |  -= Time Freeze  |  == Invinciblity
