HOW TO PLAY

GAME OBJECTIVE

  • 2-player fighting game — both players share one keyboard
  • Win 2 out of 3 rounds to claim the match
  • Reduce your opponent's health to zero to win a round instantly
  • Or have more health when the 30-second timer runs out
  • Earn coins each round — spend them in the Shop for power-ups

PLAYER 1 CONTROLS (Left Fighter)

  • A — Move Left
  • D — Move Right
  • W — Jump
  • SPACEBAR — Attack
  • SHIFT — Block (reduces any hit to 5 damage)
  • E — Special / Ultimate Attack (when charge bar is full)

PLAYER 2 CONTROLS (Right Fighter)

  • ← Arrow — Move Left
  • → Arrow — Move Right
  • ↑ Arrow — Jump
  • ↓ Arrow — Attack
  • RIGHT CTRL — Block (reduces any hit to 5 damage)
  • ENTER ↵ — Special / Ultimate Attack (when charge bar is full)

IN-GAME ITEM HOTKEYS

  • Items are offered before each round — or activate them mid-round:
  • P1 Keys: 1 = Shield  |  2 = Health Boost  |  3 = Damage Boost
  • P1 Keys: 4 = Speed Boost  |  5 = Time Freeze  |  6 = Invincibility

SPECIAL ATTACK (ULTIMATE)

  • Land hits to fill your Special bar at the bottom of the screen
  • Normal hit: +15 charge  |  Critical hit: +30 charge
  • E — Player 1 unleashes Special when bar is FULL
  • ENTER ↵ — Player 2 unleashes Special when bar is FULL
  • Special Attack deals 3× damage — 60 normal / 120 on a critical!

BEST OF 3 ROUNDS

  • First player to win 2 rounds is the match champion
  • Round dots shown beside the timer (● = round won)
  • Health, position, and special charge reset between rounds
  • Item pop-ups appear at the start of each round

COMBAT SYSTEM

  • Normal attack deals 20 damage
  • Critical hits (20% chance) deal 40 damage
  • Special Attack deals 60 damage (120 on critical!)
  • Blocking reduces any hit to only 5 damage
  • Shield item completely blocks the next hit
  • Combo counter tracks consecutive hits — try to chain them!
  • Both players start with 300 HP per round; 30-second timer

🛒 SHOP ITEMS

  • 🛡️ Shield — 100 coins: Blocks the very next hit completely
  • ❤️ Health Boost — 150 coins: Start a round with +50 extra HP
  • ⚡ Damage Boost — 200 coins: Deal 1.5× damage for the whole round
  • 🏃 Speed Boost — 175 coins: Move 70% faster for the round
  • ❄️ Time Freeze — 250 coins: Freeze the round timer for 5 seconds
  • ⭐ Invincibility — 350 coins: Immune to all damage for 4 seconds
  • You start each session with 500 coins; win rounds to earn more

⏸️ PAUSE MENU

  • ESC — Pause / Resume game
  • R — Watch instant replay of the last 5 seconds
  • M — View match statistics overlay
  • Q — Quit to main menu
  • CTRL + M — Open match stats without pausing
